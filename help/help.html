Foster FAQ

  • Q: How long is the foster period?
    A: Usually 2–6 weeks.
  • Q: Do I need experience?
    A: No, guidance is provided.
  • Q: What is the adoption process?
    A: First, fill out our online application. If approved, we'll arrange a meet-and-greet with the pet. The final step is completing the adoption agreement and paying the fee.
  • Q: How long does the adoption process take?
    A: The process typically takes 1-2 weeks, depending on the number of applications and the availability of staff to conduct home visits.
  • Q: What is the adoption fee?
    A: The adoption fee varies by pet. It helps cover the costs of vaccinations, spaying/neutering, microchipping, and initial vet checkups.
  • Q: Are all pets spayed or neutered before adoption?
    A: Yes, all pets are spayed or neutered, up-to-date on vaccinations, and microchipped before they go to their new homes.
  • Q: What if the pet doesn't work out?
    A: We require all adopted pets to be returned to our rescue if the adoption is not successful. We are committed to finding them the right home, no matter how long it takes.
  • Q: Do you conduct home visits?
    A: Yes, a home visit is a standard part of our process. It helps us ensure the pet will be in a safe environment and is a great opportunity to answer any questions you may have.
  • Q: Can I adopt a pet if I have children or other pets?
    A: Yes, but we will discuss the pet's temperament and history to ensure they are a good match for your household. We often arrange meet-and-greets to see how they get along.
  • Q: How do I meet a pet I'm interested in?
    A: After your application is approved, we will contact you to schedule a meet-and-greet. This can be at the shelter or the pet's foster home.

Volunteer with Us

Our volunteers are the heart of our mission. By giving your time, you help us provide the love, care, and attention our animals need on their journey to a forever home. No experience is necessary—just a passion for helping pets in need!

Animal Care & Socialization

Help with daily tasks like feeding, cleaning kennels, and, most importantly, spending time playing with and comforting our pets. Your presence makes a huge difference.

Adoption Event Support

Assist us at local adoption events. You'll help set up, talk to potential adopters, and handle animals to ensure a smooth and fun experience for everyone.

Administrative & Office Help

Use your professional skills to help with behind-the-scenes tasks like data entry, answering phones, managing applications, or organizing files. Every hand helps, even at a desk!
